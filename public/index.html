SRHR Myth-Busting Response Templates

🔴 Contraception & Fertility FALSE CLAIM
Claim: "Modern contraceptives cause permanent infertility in young women"
FACT: Modern contraceptives do NOT cause permanent infertility. According to WHO and Kenya MoH, fertility returns within weeks to months after stopping most contraceptive methods. Millions of Kenyan women use contraceptives safely every year. For accurate information, speak to a trained health provider or visit your nearest public health facility. #SRHRFacts #MyBodyMyRights
📊 Twitter/X: 280 chars max · WhatsApp: Full text · Facebook: Full text
🟠 Comprehensive Sexuality Education MISLEADING CLAIM
Claim: "Comprehensive sexuality education promotes promiscuity among teenagers"
FACT: Comprehensive sexuality education (CSE) does NOT promote promiscuity. Multiple peer-reviewed studies show CSE actually delays sexual debut and increases protective behaviors among young people. UNESCO and WHO endorse CSE as an evidence-based approach. Denying young people accurate information puts them at greater risk. #CSEWorks #YouthSRHR
📊 Twitter/X: 280 chars max · WhatsApp: Full text · Facebook: Full text
🔴 HIV Treatment FALSE CLAIM
Claim: "HIV can be cured by traditional herbs — doctors are hiding the cure"
FACT: There is no herbal cure for HIV. This dangerous myth causes people to delay life-saving antiretroviral therapy (ART). ART suppresses HIV to undetectable levels, allowing people to live long healthy lives and preventing transmission. If you or someone you know is HIV positive, please contact your nearest health facility for free ART. #KnowYourStatus #TreatmentWorks
📊 Twitter/X: 280 chars max · WhatsApp: Full text · Facebook: Full text
🟡 Safe Abortion Access PARTIALLY FALSE
Claim: "Safe abortion services are illegal everywhere in Kenya"
FACT: Safe abortion services are NOT illegal in all circumstances in Kenya. The Constitution of Kenya (Article 26) permits abortion when the life or health of the mother is at risk, or in an emergency. Spreading misinformation about abortion legality puts lives at risk and deters people from accessing legal, safe care. All women deserve accurate information. #AbortionIsHealthcare #KenyaConstitution

📌 Best Practices for Responding to Disinformation
1. Lead with FACT — not the false claim
2. Keep responses under 280 chars for Twitter/X
3. Always include a credible source link
4. Use hashtags consistently across the network
5. Tag @MoHKenya or @WHO_Kenya where relevant
6. Report the original post if platform allows
