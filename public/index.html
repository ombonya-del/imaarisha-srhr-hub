3 Active Alerts --- Disinformation Circulating in Network Counties
New SRHR-related false claims have been flagged this week across social media and community WhatsApp groups. Review, verify, and share corrections with your networks.
Active Alerts
Flagged: Mar 13, 2026
FALSE
"Modern contraceptives cause permanent infertility in young women"
Evidence from WHO and Kenya MoH confirms no long-term fertility impact from modern contraceptive use. Fertility typically returns within weeks of stopping most methods.
📍 Spreading: WhatsApp, TikTok 🔥 High spread
Flagged: Mar 11, 2026
MISLEADING
"Comprehensive sexuality education promotes promiscuity among teenagers"
Peer-reviewed evidence consistently shows CSE delays sexual debut and increases protective behaviors. The framing misrepresents curriculum content.
📍 Spreading: Facebook, Community radios ⚡ Medium spread
Flagged: Mar 10, 2026
FALSE
"HIV can be cured by traditional herbs --- doctors are hiding the cure"
No herbal cure for HIV exists. Antiretroviral therapy (ART) suppresses viral load to undetectable levels and enables healthy lives, but is not a cure. Delaying ART is dangerous.
📍 Spreading: YouTube, WhatsApp 🔥 High spread
Flagged: Mar 7, 2026
PARTIALLY FALSE
"Safe abortion services are illegal everywhere in Kenya"
Kenya's Constitution (Article 26) permits abortion where life or health of the mother is at risk or an emergency exists. Blanket claims of total illegality are inaccurate and deter women from accessing legal care.
📍 Spreading: Twitter/X, Community meetings • Low spread
